## EN

### How long does a build take?
It depends on scope — and we give you the real number up front. Typically: a working demo in 1–3 hours, a full website in under 24 hours, and a complete custom system in about 3 days (usually 1–3). Larger, more complex builds can take longer; we commit to your exact timeline in writing before we start.

### How much does a website cost?
We don't list fixed prices because every build is custom. Pricing depends on scope, integrations, and timeline. After the intake form we send a clear, itemized quote with no surprises. For a fast ballpark, WhatsApp +971 50 534 9907.

### Do you work with businesses outside the UAE?
Yes. Most of our current clients are UAE-based, but we work with any business that needs a premium digital system. We deliver everything remotely.

### What's included in support after launch?
Post-launch support is included on every build. Exact scope (uptime monitoring, content updates, feature additions) depends on the package. We're transparent about what's free and what's a paid extension.

### Can you build a chatbot for my business?
Yes — this is our flagship service. We build custom AI chat and WhatsApp flows that are trained on your business, your services, and your sales process. Not a generic bot. Think of the chatbot on this site as a working sample.

### Do you handle hosting and domains?
We can set up hosting and domain configuration as part of the build, or work alongside your existing setup. For most clients we deploy to Vercel and use Firebase for backend data.

### What tech stack do you use?
Frontend: modern HTML/CSS/JS, React where it helps, no heavy frameworks unless needed. Backend: Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Functions) or Vercel serverless. We choose what serves the project, not what's trendy.

### Can you redesign an existing site?
Yes. We do full rebuilds and partial redesigns. For a full rebuild we usually find it faster and cleaner to start fresh than to retrofit an old codebase.

### Do you offer SEO?
Every site we build is technically SEO-ready (clean markup, fast performance, proper meta tags). We don't offer ongoing SEO content campaigns as a standalone service.

### How do I start?
1. Fill out the intake form on this site (it takes ~3 minutes).
2. We review it and schedule a short call.
3. We send a quote and timeline.
4. Once approved, we build and ship.

Or message us directly on WhatsApp at +971 50 534 9907 — fastest path.

### What if I'm not sure exactly what I need?
That's fine — bring the problem, not the spec. The intake form has space for "I'm not sure yet." We'll figure out the right system on the call.

### Do you sign NDAs?
Yes, on request. Most of our work is public, but we're happy to sign an NDA before discussing sensitive details.
